Template Categories (Meta Official Guidelines)
📢 Marketing
Promotions, offers, launches, retargeting
Examples: sale announcements, coupons, abandoned cart, newsletters, product launches, cross-sells.
⚙️ Utility
Transaction / account updates triggered by user
Examples: order confirmation, shipping update, payment receipt, appointment reminder, account alerts.
🔐 Authentication
One-time passcodes for identity verification only
Must use Cloud API Template Library. Supports copy-code or one-tap OTP button. Marketing/Utility cannot send OTPs.
⚠️ Common Miscategorization Mistakes What Meta Does
Adding "Check out our new items" to a receipt (Utility template) Auto-reclassified as Marketing → higher cost
Including promotional CTAs inside support/service messages Template rejected or reclassified
Using Utility template to send OTP / verification code Blocked — only Authentication category allowed
Promotional intent not 'specific to user action' Fails utility test → Marketing category
April 2025 Enforcement: If a business selects UTILITY but Meta determines it should be MARKETING, the template is auto-approved as MARKETING (different pricing). For businesses abusing the categorization system, the prior 24-hour notice before reclassification is NO LONGER given — effective April 16, 2025.

7
2026 Key Updates & Compliance
  • 🚀 Fast-Track to 100K Limit Once a business completes Business Verification or the Quality scaling path, the 100K daily messaging limit is granted immediately. Rollout began Q1 2026, with pacing restrictions fully removed Q2 2026.
  • 🚫 General Purpose AI Chatbots Banned Since January 15, 2026, Meta has banned General Purpose AI Chatbots on WhatsApp to keep the platform focused as a business channel. Business-specific AI is still allowed.
  • 🇺🇸 US (+1) Marketing Templates Blocked Since April 2025, marketing templates to US +1 numbers are not delivered. Utility and authentication templates continue to work normally.
  • ⏱️ Per-User Marketing Cap Select countries enforce a limit of 1 marketing template per 24 hours to the same recipient. Combined with global frequency capping (~2 marketing msgs/day total).
  • ⚠️ Category Mismatch = Rejection Promotional content inside Utility templates will be rejected. Template category must match the actual content — Meta actively enforces this.
