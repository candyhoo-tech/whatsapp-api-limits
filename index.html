Template Categories (Meta Official Guidelines)模板類別（Meta 官方指南）
📢 Marketing 行銷
Promotions, offers, launches, retargeting
Examples: sale announcements, coupons, abandoned cart, newsletters, product launches, cross-sells.範例：促銷通知、優惠券、購物車挽回、電子報、新品上架、交叉推薦。
⚙️ Utility 實用
Transaction / account updates triggered by user
Examples: order confirmation, shipping update, payment receipt, appointment reminder, account alerts.範例：訂單確認、出貨通知、付款收據、預約提醒、帳戶異動通知。
🔐 Authentication 驗證
One-time passcodes for identity verification only
Must use Cloud API Template Library. Supports copy-code or one-tap OTP button. Marketing/Utility cannot send OTPs.必須使用 Cloud API Template Library，支援複製驗證碼或一鍵驗證按鈕。行銷／實用類模板不可發送 OTP。
⚠️ Common Miscategorization Mistakes 常見分類錯誤 What Meta Does Meta 的處理方式
Adding "Check out our new items" to a receipt (Utility template)在收據（實用模板）加入「查看最新商品」字樣 Auto-reclassified as Marketing → higher cost自動歸類為行銷 → 費用提高
Including promotional CTAs inside support/service messages在客服／服務訊息中加入促銷連結或按鈕 Template rejected or reclassified模板被駁回或重新分類
Using Utility template to send OTP / verification code使用實用模板發送 OTP／驗證碼 Blocked — only Authentication allowed封鎖 — 僅驗證類模板可發送
Promotional intent not 'specific to user action'內容不符「針對用戶動作觸發」的規則 Fails utility test → Marketing category無法通過實用類驗證 → 歸為行銷
April 2025 Enforcement: If a business selects UTILITY but Meta determines it should be MARKETING, the template is auto-approved as MARKETING (different pricing). For businesses abusing the categorization system, the prior 24-hour notice before reclassification is NO LONGER given — effective April 16, 2025. 若商家選擇 UTILITY 但 Meta 判定為 MARKETING，模板會直接改以 MARKETING 核准（收費不同）。對於濫用分類系統的商家，自 2025 年 4 月 16 日起，不再提供先前的 24 小時預告。

2026 Key Updates & Compliance2026 重要更新與合規事項
  • 🚀 Fast-Track to 100K Limit 直升 100K 訊息上限 Once a business completes Business Verification or the Quality scaling path, the 100K daily messaging limit is granted immediately. Rollout began Q1 2026, with pacing restrictions fully removed Q2 2026. 完成商家驗證或通過品質擴展路徑後，可立即獲得 10 萬則／日上限。2026 第一季開始推出，第二季全面取消 pacing 限制。
  • 🚫 General Purpose AI Chatbots Banned 禁止通用型 AI 聊天機器人 Since January 15, 2026, Meta has banned General Purpose AI Chatbots on WhatsApp to keep the platform focused as a business channel. Business-specific AI is still allowed. 自 2026 年 1 月 15 日起，Meta 禁止通用型 AI 聊天機器人進駐 WhatsApp，以維持其商業通訊定位。特定商家用途的 AI 仍允許使用。
  • 🇺🇸 US (+1) Marketing Templates Blocked 美國（+1）行銷模板封鎖中 Since April 2025, marketing templates to US +1 numbers are not delivered. Utility and authentication templates continue to work normally. 自 2025 年 4 月起，發送至美國 +1 號碼的行銷模板將無法送達。實用型與驗證型模板仍可正常使用。
  • ⏱️ Per-User Marketing Cap 每位用戶行銷訊息上限 Select countries enforce a limit of 1 marketing template per 24 hours to the same recipient. Combined with global frequency capping (~2 marketing msgs/day total). 部分國家規定同一收件人 24 小時內僅能收到 1 則行銷模板，再加上全球頻率限制（每日合計約 2 則行銷訊息）。
  • ⚠️ Category Mismatch = Rejection 類別不符 = 自動駁回 Promotional content inside Utility templates will be rejected. Template category must match the actual content — Meta actively enforces this. 若實用型模板內含促銷內容，將被自動駁回。模板類別必須與實際內容相符，Meta 會嚴格審查。
